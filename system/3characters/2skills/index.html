Skills


Every character has access to 11 distinct skills, covering most, if not all possible actions they could take during the game. They can be roughly divided into physical, mental and social skills, there is however some overlap, as often multiple skills can be used for certain actions, based on the players descriptions of their characters activities.

All skills range from 0 to 5 and determine the number of d8 rolled in a check of the required skill. A skill with 0 does not roll 0d8, but instead 1d6.

During character creation all skills start with a 1 and can be increased to a certain degree. During the game they can be increased further by training the skills area of expertise or by choosing specific talents that increase them.

Physical skills
  • Agility Use this skill when you perform dangerous physical activities like swimming, jumping or climbing and when you use physical strength or speed and power.
  • Skirmish Use this skill for combat and fighting checks, handling weapons and harming or killing enemies.
  • Trickery Use this skill when you want to hide, sneak and be stealthy. You can also use it for pickpocketing, lockpicking and everything, that requires a steady hand.

Mental skills
  • Lore Use this skill to recall knowledge, process information and draw conclusions.
  • Crafting Use this skill to create and modify, as well as repair items and gadgets and invent your own recipes. With the right specialization you can use it to create complex inventions, such as mutations, mechanical marvels or magical potions.
  • Vigilance Use this skill to perceive your surroundings and look for danger, illusions, hints and things that stand out.

Social skills
  • Influence Use this skill to sway and convince, lie or persuade people and make them do your bidding.
  • Insight Use this skill to read people and situations, see through arcane effects and other deceptions and use it to look inward to find yourself.
  • Performance Use this skill to sing, dance, act and disguise convincingly and with ease.

The Magic skill
  • Magic Use this skill to wield and understand magic and withstand magical effects aimed at you. You gain additional maximum threads equal this skill.
