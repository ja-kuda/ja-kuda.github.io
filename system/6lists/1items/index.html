Weapons

  • Shimmering dagger - dagger - While in direct sunlight, this dagger becomes almost impossible to see. Gain a boon when using this dagger in open sunlight.
  • Boomerang sword - sword, boomerang - This curved sword can be thrown like a boomerang. When thrown G{the sword returns to your hand}
  • Flicker dagger - dagger - 3 charges, You can teleport to the position of the dagger, holding it in your hand upon arriving.
  • Whispering bow - bow - This gnarled bow constantly whispers into the wielders ears, marking enemies weakspots for them. Gain a boon when attacking a marked enemy with the bow. The whispering is distracting however, as long as you have the bow on you, gain a flaw to concentration.
  • Staff of growth - staff - 2 charges, You can use the staff to create plants and roots in the vicinity.
  • Silent crossbow - crossbow - This crossbow is completely silent at all times.
  • Binding of swordsaac - sword - G{Bind your enemy with ghostly chains, immobilizing them.}
  • Shield of reflection - shield - 3 armor, you can expend 1 armor when you are targeted by a ranged attack or projectile to reflect the attack back at the attacker with a skirmish check.
  • Phoenix feather - spear - 1 charge, summon a small phoenix companion for a few minutes. The spear inflicts fire damage.
  • Shadowstalker - shortsword - This small sword can pass through solid objects. When used within shadows it grants a boon on skirmish checks.
  • Stormcaller - staff - 1 charge, This staff can summon a localized thunderstorm that shoots lightning at all creatures within it.
  • Heartseeker - longbow - 1 charge, costs 1 resolve, You make a true shot at a target, always killing any normal creature.
  • Frostbite - heavy axe - Deals ice damage. G{Freeze your target solid}
  • Thunderhammer - light hammer - Instead of dealing damage on a skirmish check, this hammer can create a loud thunderclap on impact, disorienting all creatures in the vicinity. Deals electric damage.
  • Poison dagger - dagger - This small dagger is hollow and can be filled discreetly with a variety of poisons that are inflicted on its targets.
  • Windspeaker - shortbow - A whimsical bow that does not shoot arrows but instead shoots blasts of concentrated air, dealing no damage, but knocking back its targets based on their weight.
  • Clobfusion - heavy club - This heavy club leaves foes sluggish and confused after a hit.
  • Shield of shadows - light shield - 2 charges, Create a small area of shadow that lasts for a short duration.
